Fantastic

Beasts

The Fantastic Beasts franchise has been beset by plenty of bad luck — some of its own making and some beyond its control. All of this has meant that the journey to the third Fantastic Beasts film has been bumpy, to say the least. Fantastic Beasts 3, along with many other movies meant to be filming in 2020, was hit with production delays, but that wasn't the most dramatic news to hit. That mantle goes to the news that Grindelwald actor Johnny Depp would depart the series. The third film was originally meant to debut in cinemas in November 2020 (yep!) but even before These Unprecedented Times™ it was delayed to 2021, before being delayed again.

That we're getting a third movie at all is the stuff of magic. Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald met with poor reviews and garnered the worst box office result of the entire Wizarding World series. This was likely not what Warner Bros had in mind when it planned a five-movie series for Fantastic Beasts... so will we end up getting all five films?
